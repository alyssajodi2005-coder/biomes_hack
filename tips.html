Daily support

Small habits make rehab easier to stick with.

Keep your form clean, your routine consistent, and your knee calmer during recovery.

Tips for today

Keep rehab steady

1

Move slow and controlled so your knee stays aligned through each rep.

2

Use pain as a guide and scale back if swelling or instability increases.

3

Stack your rehab routine at the same time each day to stay consistent.

4

Ask your therapist before adding jumping, twisting, or cutting drills.

Consistency

Easy routine cues

  • Do rehab after the same meal or class every day
  • Keep a towel, band, and ice pack in one place
  • Write down what movements feel strongest this week
